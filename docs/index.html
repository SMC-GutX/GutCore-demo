Study in Brief

GutCore was pretrained on 5.6 million de-identified endoscopic images. The study tested whether all images from one endoscopic examination could be aggregated for cancer detection, invasion-depth assessment, biomarker-associated phenotypes, and survival stratification.

Public image-level datasets were used as supporting benchmarks. GutCore remains a retrospective research model and is not intended for clinical decision-making without further validation.
